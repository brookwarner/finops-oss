A few rules

Clear space

Keep padding equal to the bar's height on all sides. Inside an app tile the mark occupies ~62% of the square — don't let it touch the corner radius.

Don't

Don't recolor the segments, swap their order (green is always in, left), add a gradient, or set the mark on a busy photo without the solid tile behind it.
